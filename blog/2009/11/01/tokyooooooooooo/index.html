Tokyo Drift

Nov 1st, 2009

Windows update automatically updated my computer and did a restart which meant that alarmclock.com was no longer on, so my alarm never went off. Luckily, I woke up at 6:30 AM. How my body knew to only sleep for 3.5 hours when it was totally wasted from the day before is beyond me. I grabbed my stuff, checked out as quickly as possible and hopped in a cab to the airport and told the driver to step on it. This time when I got to the airport, rather than patiently waiting, I ran to the front of a line and asked if I could still get on my flight. Luckily the taxi ride to the airport was only about 30 minutes instead of the hour I had been told. So I made my flight with about 15 minutes to spare. So lucky. I think if I had missed that flight, I would have just had to give up on this whole trip and go home. I really think it would have broken my spirit. The highlight of the day was an incredible sunrise where the sun just appeared as a golden ball on the horizon. Don’t think I’ve ever really seen the sun rise like that, must be the smog or something.

I checked into a hostel as Tokyo is crazy expensive for hotels and I figured it’d make it easier to meet people. Although update:everyone at this hostel is really unfriendly. I have not met one person yet. Anyways, I was so exhausted that I just grabbed a bite to eat and hit the sack despite really wanting to go out on a Friday night in Tokyo.[gallery]
